Steak

Description

Steak is a popular and delicious cut of meat that is typically obtained from beef,
although it can also be made from other animals like pork, lamb, or even fish

Ingredients

  • beef chops
  • onions
  • butter

steps

  • melt the butter on a hot pan
  • fry the onions
  • add the steak and fry until the crust is formed
  • let it marinate for a few
