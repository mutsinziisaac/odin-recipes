chicken curry

Description

Chicken curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich and spiced sauce.
It is a popular dish in many cuisines around the world,
particularly in Indian, Thai, and Caribbean cuisine

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • water
  • butter

steps

  • melt the butter on a hot pan
  • fry the onions and the tomatoes
  • add the chicken breasts and let it marrinate
